Granting Facebook Ads Access

How to add Playbook Marketing as a Partner on your Meta Business Manager — in about 5 minutes.

Get started

Granting access through Meta Business Manager keeps your accounts, Pages, and Pixel under your ownership. We only receive permissions to manage them on your behalf, and you can revoke access in one click.

Step by step

How to add us as a Partner

  1. Open Meta Business Settings

    Go to business.facebook.com/settings and sign in. If you manage multiple businesses, use the switcher in the top-left.

  2. Go to Users → Partners

    In the left menu, scroll to Users → Partners.

  3. Click Add → Give a partner access to your assets

    Click the blue Add button, then Give a partner access to your assets.

  4. Paste our Business ID

    Paste our Meta Business ID — 1506793077364799 — and click Next. Playbook Marketing will appear as a pending partner.

  5. Share the right assets

    Select and share: Page (Manage Page), Ad Account (Manage ad account), Pixel (Manage Pixel), and Instagram (Manage Instagram) if applicable.

  6. Save and notify us

    Click Save Changes. Reply to your onboarding email and we'll accept the connection within one business day.

Don't have a Business Manager yet?

Visit business.facebook.com/overview and click Create account. It's free and takes three minutes. Then come back and follow the steps above.

Before we begin

Confirmation checklist

Tick each item as you complete it. Your progress is saved automatically. When everything's checked, reply to your onboarding email and we'll verify on our side.

If something's off

Common issues

I don't see the Partners option.

You may not have a Business Manager. Go to business.facebook.com/overview and click Create account, then retry.

Meta says I'm not the owner of the ad account.

The ad account must be claimed into your Business Manager first. Go to Accounts → Ad Accounts → Add and choose Claim.

Nothing happened after I added your Business ID.

Partner connections require acceptance from our side. We'll accept within one business day. You can verify the request is pending under Users → Partners.
